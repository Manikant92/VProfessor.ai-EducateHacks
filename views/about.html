Who Are We?

VProfessor.ai contains a multitude of functions designed to facilitate online learning. Users can upload either a video file or audio file. A transcript of the audio is returned, along with a summary of the data. This includes key words and main topics, Wikipedia page links, current events, and recommended YouTube videos. From here, users can either read the summary report on the website, or download it as a pdf for their personal studying. Additionally, users may "ask the professor" a question, and get a quick short answer for themselves.
