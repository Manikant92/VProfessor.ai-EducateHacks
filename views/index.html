How It Works

Simply upload your classwork or lecture in one of the supported file formats, and wait a little, then you will receive your notes! Your file is sent to a back end where we run multiple NLP algorithms that break down your work into a format understandable by the computer before analyzing it. From there we annotate your work and scrape the web for knowledge on your topic before finally presenting it to you!

Our Mission

With the COVID-19 pandemic plunging people into online school, educating students and learning has become more difficult than ever. To address this issue, we created a quick and easy source to easily obtain more issue on the topics that you are learning and/or teaching so you can work more efficiently.
